Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2025

At byteBANK, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our digital banking services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification (name, phone number, email address)
  • Financial information (bank account, card details, transaction history)

  • Location data (with your consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide secure access to your accounts
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement strong encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

4. Sharing of Data

We never sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third parties who process it on our behalf for customer support, legal compliance, or fraud prevention — always under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
